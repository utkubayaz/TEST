MY LIFE

I was born in Anamur and lived there until I was 19 years old. My mother and my father are teachers. My mother is a primary school teacher, my father is a geography teacher and I have a sister.

Anamur is although there are places of historical interest, long beaches and a clean sea there is no large scale tourism on this part of the coast as the coast road in both directions is winding and really slow to drive..
